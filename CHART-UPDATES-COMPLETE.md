2. **Clear comparison** between "First 7 Days" vs "By Launch"
3. **80% target line** provides context for success
4. **Incentive markers** explain performance changes
5. **Side-by-side layout** allows easy scanning

### Alternative Options You Could Consider:

**Option A: Stacked Bars**
- Show 80% and 100% as stacked bars
- Visually shows 100% as subset of 80%
- More compact but harder to read exact numbers

**Option B: Line Charts**
- Show trends over time instead of bars
- Better for seeing overall trajectory
- Loses some impact of individual campaign performance

**Option C: Combined Chart**
- All 4 metrics on one chart
- Very compact
- Can become cluttered with too much information

### 🎯 **Recommendation: Keep Current Layout**

Your current setup with:
- 3 separate focused charts
- Clear grouping (First 7 Days vs By Launch)
- Target lines and milestone markers

This provides the **best balance** of:
- ✅ Clarity
