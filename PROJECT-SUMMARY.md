## 📊 Comparison Metrics Explained

### For Email Newsletters (Weekly Wrap & SMO Connect)

The dashboard provides **3 types of comparisons**:

#### 📅 Year-over-Year (YoY)
- Compares current period to **same period last year**
- Example: This January vs Last January
- Shows long-term trend

#### 📆 Quarter-over-Quarter (QoQ)
- Compares current quarter average to **previous quarter average**
- Example: Q1 2025 vs Q4 2024
- Shows seasonal trends

#### 📊 Month-over-Month (MoM)
- Compares current month/week to **previous month/week**
- Example: This week vs last week
- Shows immediate trends
